# Tatwir Talent HRMS Overview

Tatwir Talent is a multi-tenant, AI-driven HR and workforce management platform for deskless teams. The system focuses on scheduling, performance analytics, payroll automation, and audit-grade tracking.

## Core Modules
- Multi-tenant organization management with strict data isolation
- Role-based access for Super Admin, Admin, Team Leader, Employee
- Employee, department, location, and contract management
- Shift scheduling with conflict detection
- Event tracking (check-in, movement, sales) with JSON payloads
- KPI and analytics dashboards
- Payroll engine with KPI-based adjustments
- Tracking and geofencing (design-ready)
- AI insights and anomaly detection (mocked)
- LMS onboarding and course milestones
- Audit logs for all actions
